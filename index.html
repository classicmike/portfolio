about-me

Hi I'm Mike. I am an aspiring <frontend></developer> who eats HTML and CSS for breakfast, Javascript for lunch and Responsive Web Design for dinner.

I also take light snacks of PHP and C# in between.

In the middle of it all, I rage over the following things:

  • The inconsistency of browsers
  • Layouts breaking over different edge cases, for devices and screens
  • Taking over somebody else's code which is poorly written and documented. Myself included :P
  • The fast evolution of frameworks which results in me having to learn a new framework again, just when I have mastered my previous one.
  • Having to convince a client that using animated gifs, midi files and blindingly high-contrast colour schemes is not the best way to show their brand on their website.
  • Unrealistic deadlines
  • The designer behind my back telling me to move elements, to the left, to the right, make things bigger, make things smaller, yada yada yada...

To power me through all the rage and more, I go for long guelling fast-paced runs, and fuel up on plenty of Red Bull (My pair of wings).

Through all the madness I've come to appreciate the work that I do in frontend web development, making websites and web apps that look awesome functional on all popular devices, tablets and mobiles, whilst keeping my work clean and easy to read & follow for others to use and reuse.

One way or another I am going to make my mark, lead or be part of team to make a major contribution to the frontend of a product that is loved by millions around the world.
